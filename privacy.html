1. Who We Are

CheckMyGrammar ("we", "us", "our") operates the website checkmygrammar.io — a free online grammar and spell checking tool for students, professionals and everyday writers.

By using CheckMyGrammar, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of information necessary to provide our service:

Text You Enter

When you type or paste text into the CheckMyGrammar editor, that text is sent to the LanguageTool API for grammar and spell analysis. We do not store, log or retain any text you enter into the editor. Your writing is sent for analysis only and is never saved on our servers.

Email Address (Optional)

If you choose to join our Pro waitlist, we collect your email address. This is entirely optional. We use this email only to notify you when CheckMyGrammar Pro launches. We will never sell, rent or share your email with third parties.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymised usage data such as pages visited, time on site, browser type and country. This data cannot be used to identify you personally and helps us improve the service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate the grammar checking service
  • To send Pro waitlist notifications (if you signed up)
  • To understand how users interact with our site so we can improve it
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for:

  • Selling or renting to third parties
  • Targeting you with personalised advertising based on your text content
  • Building profiles or tracking you across other websites

4. Third-Party Services

CheckMyGrammar uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

LanguageTool API

Your text is sent to LanguageTool's servers for grammar and spelling analysis. LanguageTool processes text transiently and does not store it permanently. View their privacy policy at languagetool.org/legal/privacy.

Google Analytics (if enabled)

We may use Google Analytics to collect anonymised data about how visitors use our site. Google Analytics uses cookies. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. View Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Google AdSense (if enabled)

We may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised ads at adssettings.google.com. View Google's advertising privacy policy at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Vercel (Hosting)

Our website is hosted on Vercel, which may collect server logs including IP addresses. View Vercel's privacy policy at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. CheckMyGrammar uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function correctly (e.g. remembering your cookie consent choice)
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the site (anonymised). Used only with your consent.
  • Advertising cookies: Used by Google AdSense to show relevant ads. Used only with your consent.

You can manage or withdraw your cookie consent at any time by clicking the cookie settings button at the bottom of any page. You can also control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Advertising

CheckMyGrammar is a free service and may display advertisements to support its operation. We use Google AdSense to serve ads.

Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your previous visits to our site or other sites on the internet. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

We do not control the content of advertisements displayed by Google, but we are committed to maintaining a positive, non-intrusive user experience.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to deletion: Request that we delete your personal data
  • Right to correction: Request that we correct inaccurate data
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Right to object: Object to processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@checkmygrammar.io.

If you are located in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). If you are located in California, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary:

  • Text you check: Not retained — deleted immediately after analysis
  • Email address (waitlist): Retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Analytics data: Retained for up to 26 months in anonymised form

9. Children's Privacy

CheckMyGrammar is suitable for use by students of all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at contact@checkmygrammar.io and we will delete that information promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of CheckMyGrammar after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: contact@checkmygrammar.io
  • Website: checkmygrammar.io

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 5 business days.
